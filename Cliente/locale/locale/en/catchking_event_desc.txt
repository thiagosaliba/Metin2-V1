GAME RULES[ENTER]
[ENTER]
Hunt down monsters to collect King Cards. Together 25 King Cards make[ENTER]
up a King Deck, with which you can play a game of Catch the King.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
[ENTER]
AIM OF THE GAME[ENTER]
[ENTER]
Reveal as many cards as possible and collect points. Catch the king[ENTER]
in one go on the very last move to pick up 100 points at once. If you[ENTER]
earn at least 10 points, you can redeem a prize at the end of the[ENTER]
game.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
You can wager up to 5 King Decks each time you play, earning the same[ENTER]
number of chests in reward. Please note: The number of cards you play[ENTER]
with is fixed; it is not influenced by the number of decks you wager.[ENTER]
Once the game has started, you cannot reclaim the King Decks you have[ENTER]
wagered.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
[ENTER]
HOW TO PLAY[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ You always start with the lowest card. An arrow indicates which[ENTER]
card can be selected.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ By clicking on the card it will appear in the 'My Card' slot. In[ENTER]
subsequent turns, cards of the same value will be moved[ENTER]
automatically.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ Turn over any face-down card on the game board and compare it to[ENTER]
your own.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If your card is greater, you will receive points equal to the value[ENTER]
of the revealed card and you can turn over another card on the board.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If the cards are the same value, you receive the points and your[ENTER]
turn is over. Play continues with a new card from your deck.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If your card's value is lower, you don't receive any points. Your[ENTER]
turn is over and play continues with a new card.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If your card is a 5 and there is a face-down 5 neighbouring the[ENTER]
card you reveal, your card will be captured. Your turn is over and[ENTER]
you don't receive any points.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If your card is the king (K) and you catch the king on the board in[ENTER]
exactly one move, you receive 100 points. The game ends after this[ENTER]
move.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ Bingo! If you reveal all cards in a row, you receive 10 extra[ENTER]
points.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If you've earned at least 10 points, you can collect your prize by[ENTER]
clicking on 'Your Reward'.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
[ENTER]
CARDS[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ Cards with the value 1 capture: 1[ENTER]
+ Cards with the value 2 capture: 1, 2[ENTER]
+ Cards with the value 3 capture: 1, 2, 3[ENTER]
+ Cards with the value 4 capture: 1, 2, 3, 4[ENTER]
+ Cards with the value 5 capture: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5[ENTER]
+ Cards with the value K capture: K[ENTER]
[ENTER]
[ENTER]
SCORE[ENTER]
[ENTER]
Revealed card is 1: +10 points[ENTER]
Revealed card is 2: +20 points[ENTER]
Revealed card is 3: +30 points[ENTER]
Revealed card is 4: +40 points[ENTER]
Revealed card is 5: +50 points[ENTER]
Revealed card is K: +100 points[ENTER]
Revealing all cards in a row: +10 points[ENTER]
[ENTER]

IMPORTANT[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ More King Decks may be found among the King's Loot. Make sure to[ENTER]
open the prize before the end of the event, as all King Cards and[ENTER]
Decks will then disappear and you'll come away empty-handed.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
